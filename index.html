The idea behind edupresence

Experience the future of attendance management with our innovative system

Experience the future of attendance management with our innovative system that seamlessly combines cutting-edge BLE Bluetooth technology with cloud integration, revolutionizing how attendance is tracked. Our smart solution eliminates traditional hassles by allowing students to log in via their Uni ID and password on our intuitive mobile portal/app, effortlessly marking their presence with a tap and Bluetooth activation. What sets us apart is our integration with cloud platforms like AWS, ensuring secure data storage and real-time insights for educators. Our BLE beacon's intelligent coding enhances security, permitting only authorized devices and guaranteeing precise attendance capture. Embrace innovation with us and transform attendance recording in educational institutions for enhanced accountability and transparency.
